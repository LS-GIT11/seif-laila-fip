Winter Wonderland Weekends

Summary of our Facilities

Experience the magic of winter for Couple’s Resort’s 58th anniversary! This December, escape to a romantic Winter Wonderland nestled in the heart of nature. Every weekend, indulge in festive activities, cozy moments, and exclusive offers crafted just for you and your loved one.

Don’t miss this chance, as the Winter Wonderland Weekends are a once in a life time opportunity only available this year!

Step into a winter wonderland and rediscover the joy of the season with curated experiences that combine adventure, relaxation, and romance. Begin your evening with a guided snowshoe hike through the resort’s pristine trails, illuminated by the soft glow of lanterns and starlight. Your guide will share fascinating stories about the local wildlife and the history of the area, adding a touch of enchantment to your journey through the snow. Afterward, trade your snowshoes for skates and glide hand-in-hand with your partner on our private rink. Surrounded by twinkling lights and softly playing holiday tunes, the rink provides the perfect setting for magical moments. Need a break? Cozy up together with steaming mugs of rich hot cocoa topped with whipped cream, served rinkside. End your evening with a fireside gathering under the open sky, where you can toast gourmet marshmallows and craft your own perfect s’mores. Each moment is designed to create lasting memories of warmth, love, and laughter in the crisp winter air.

Elevate your weekend with a dining experience that captures the spirit of the holidays and the essence of luxury. Each Saturday, our executive chef will delight your senses with a specially crafted four-course dinner inspired by the season. Begin your culinary journey with a velvety winter squash bisque or a crisp salad featuring candied pecans and cranberries. Transition into hearty mains like herb-crusted lamb or pan-seared trout, perfectly paired with seasonal sides. For dessert, indulge in a decadent chocolate fondant or a spiced apple tart that evokes the comfort of a fireside evening. Every dish is meticulously prepared using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring both quality and sustainability. To set the mood, you’ll be welcomed with a complimentary glass of mulled wine or warm spiced cider, a fragrant blend of cinnamon, cloves, and citrus that fills the air with holiday cheer. Whether you’re toasting to a special moment or simply enjoying each other’s company, this dining experience promises to be an unforgettable highlight of your stay.

Make your weekend retreat even sweeter with exclusive offers designed to enhance your Winter Wonderland experience. When you book a two-night stay, enjoy 25% off your second night, giving you more time to relax and soak in the season’s charm. For those who act quickly, bookings made before November 30th will be rewarded with a complimentary upgrade to a luxurious lake-view suite. Imagine waking up to panoramic views of a tranquil, snow-draped landscape as sunlight sparkles off the icy waters. These premium suites also feature cozy fireplaces, perfect for snuggling up after a day of exploring, and spa-inspired bathrooms that add a touch of indulgence to your stay. With limited availability, these exclusive perks are your chance to elevate your escape to the next level. Don’t miss the opportunity to wrap yourself in luxury and romance this holiday season—reserve your Winter Wonderland weekend now!
